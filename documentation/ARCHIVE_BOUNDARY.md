# Archive Boundary

This package preserves the public, aggregate-derived artifacts available locally for Janson, Levinson. (2014). HOT Or Not: Driver Elasticity To Price On The MnPASS HOT Lanes. RTE 44:21–32 (2014). 10.1016/j.retrec.2014.04.008.

## Included

The derived workbook contains aggregate figures/tables used for the paper, including toll-rate schedules, willingness-to-pay summaries, elasticity summaries, and observed/simulation comparison tables. Non-empty sheets are exported to CSV for easier reuse.

## Excluded

Source MnPASS operational/transponder logs are outside the package boundary. The package also excludes paper drafts, review responses, presentations, LaTeX build products, the manuscript-source RAR archive, and figure-only duplicates.
